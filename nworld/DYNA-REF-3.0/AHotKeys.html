Hot Keys

N-Dynamics supports a variety of hot keys, single key commands that perform an operation.

Table A.1 N-Dynamics Hot Keys
Hot Key Description
. (period)

 Remember the channel or cue under the mouse for copying or moving times or values.

1

 Set to single view.

=

 Refit all curves under this channel.

a

 Activate or deactivate channel under mouse.

b

 Animate previous frame (from last animated frame)

C

 Cut this channel.

c

 Copy remembered channel to channel under mouse.

D

 Delete channel under mouse

E

 Empty channel under mouse

f

 Freezes cues in time, giving them fixed offsets from channel

F

 Unfreeze cues in time, making them proportional to channel size.

g

 Toggle grid on and off.

h

 Hot key help.

l

 Link to remembered marker.

m

 Move remembered channel to channel under mouse.

n

 Animate next frame (from last animated frame)

O

 Unoffset cues in time, making them proportional to channel size.

o

 Set time mode of cues to fixed offset.

R

 Recompute derived curve.

r

 Reorder subchannels of channel under mouse.

s

 Insert subchannels or cues for action or curve under mouse.

T

 Trace the channel under the mouse.

t

 Set timeline to channel under the mouse.

u

 Select active modules.

v

 Open or close channel under mouse

V

 Perform Views operations.

w

 Set time of this cue to remembered time.

x

 Select previous script.
